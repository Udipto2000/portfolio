Who Am I?

I am a current John Jay College Graduate Student who is majoring in Digital Forensics and Cybersecurity

I am a Future Cybersecurity Specialist

As a former participant of the CUNY Tech Talent Pipeline Program, I developed Web Development and Web Moderating skills. A cosmopolitan at heart and an aspiring adventurer, I was born in Chittagong, Bangladesh, and grew up in New York, United States. After graduating from Townsend Harris High School, I decided to attend John Jay College (JJC) because I aspire to be a cyber-security professional and prevent cybercrime. Some of my strengths include being curious, accountable, and resilient. As I look forward to expanding my horizon and applying lessons learned in classrooms in real-life scenarios, my career aspiration will continue to lie in utilizing cyber-security to serve people around the world whether in the financial sector, preventing cyberattacks, or in the government sector, fighting cyberterrorism.
